Background

The lack of information exchange between customers and suppliers leads to overproduction, high levels of stock and overtime.
Furthermore, it can result in a loss of customers and additional transportation costs. The quality of planning and handling of information
can significantly influence these key aspects and therefore potentially increase the competitiveness of Austrian companies in the global market.

This tool was created as a part of the InnoFIT project, which aims at developing innovative forecasting tools for improved production planning. The project is funded by the Austrian Research Promotion Agency (FFG) and runs from 1 June 2018 until 31 May 2021.
